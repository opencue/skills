# Composite voices — reusable persona library

Fourteen pre-defined composite personas for use in `qa-interview` and `sector-analysis` presets. Each voice has fixed geography, role, specialty, and tone. **Do not drift personas off-axis.** If a topic needs a voice that isn't here, add a new one — don't reshape an existing one.

All voices are **explicitly composite**. Articles using these must include the standard composite-voices disclaimer in frontmatter (see `SKILL.md`).

## How to use these in an article

Pick 2-3 voices per `qa-interview`. Pick 1-2 voices per `sector-analysis`. **Always cite the voice on first mention**:

> The voices:
>
> - **Mira T.**, principal at an Asia-based multi-family office (HKD-denominated book, ~USD 2.4B AUM, less than 5% in digital assets).

After first mention, just use the first name in attribution: "Mira T.:".

Voices may discuss topics outside their core specialty IF the topic is adjacent. They may NOT pivot wholesale — a `defense-procurement` voice should not opine on EM FX. If the topic requires a voice you don't have, **add a new one** to this file and reuse it.
